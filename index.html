About PennGuard Public Safety

Your Safety, Our Commitment, Community First

PennGuard Public Safety is a organization dedicated to strengthening community safety by offering professional unarmed security services. Our trained personnel focus on vigilance, respectful engagement, and building trust between security officers and the communities they serve. From event security to regular patrols, PennGuard is committed to protecting people and property with integrity and a community-first approach.

Comprehensive Security Solutions for Events, Properties, and Public Spaces

PennGuard Public Safety provides professional, unarmed security services tailored to your event and mobile patrol needs. Our trained personnel prioritize safety, vigilance, and building trust within the community.

Unarmed Security

Skilled unarmed security personnel delivering vigilant surveillance, access control, and crowd management services for a variety of settings.

Event Security

Customized security planning and on-site protection for public and private events, ensuring safety and smooth operations.

Patrol Services

Regular patrols and monitoring of commercial and residential properties to deter crime and respond promptly to incidents.
